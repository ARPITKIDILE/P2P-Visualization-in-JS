BIT TORRENT LIKE APPLICATION

On Starting of exe file due to unregistered version a dialog appears.Close the dialog to start the application.

Initially the network consists of four clients and one server.

Considering file is divided in four parts(Blue,green,red,yellow)

Initially clicking on step1 green and yellow packets are transferred to two clients.

On clicking step2 the two clients who have green and yellow packets share the packets with other two clients.

In this way all clients have two parts of the file green packet and yellow packet.

On clicking on step3 red and blue packets are transferred to two clients.

On clicking step4 the two clients who have red and blue packets share the packets with other two clients.

In this manner each client gets complete file in the form of four packets.
